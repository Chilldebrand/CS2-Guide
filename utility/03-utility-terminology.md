# Utility terminology

- **Default:** a spread used to gather information and preserve options.
- **Exec:** a coordinated group of grenades and player movement that takes a site.
- **Pop flash:** a flash that bursts close to the fight with little warning.
- **One-way:** a smoke position that lets one player see while the other side cannot; it is situational, not a magic defense.
- **Re-smoke:** replacing a fading smoke to preserve control.
- **Stall:** utility used to delay a hit until teammates arrive.
- **Clear:** a grenade used to remove or force a player from a position.
- **Anti-flash:** turning away or using cover so your own team’s flash can create space without blinding you.
- **Post-plant utility:** grenades saved for the defuse, retake route, or final duel.
