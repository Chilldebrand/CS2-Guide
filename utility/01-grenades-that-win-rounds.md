# Grenades that win rounds

## Smoke

Use a smoke to block a sightline, isolate a defender, force a detour, or buy time. A smoke is strongest when a teammate acts while the defender cannot see the action.

## Flash

Use a flash to make a fight fair or to move through a space. Tell the teammate whether to swing behind it, wait for the pop, or use it as a one-way information test. Avoid blind-flashing teammates without a call.

## Molotov / incendiary

Use fire to deny a position, delay a rush, clear a close corner, or force a player into a predictable exit. On post-plant, place it where it makes the defuse or the retake path uncomfortable.

## HE

Use an HE to finish a tagged player, punish a tight choke, or combine with a molotov to clear a common hold. Do not throw it randomly at the start of every round.
