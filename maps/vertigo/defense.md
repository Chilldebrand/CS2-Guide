# Vertigo — defense plan

1. Use early Ramp utility with a retreat plan; a solo anchor is a donation.
2. Keep one defender able to help B while Ramp info is uncertain.
3. On retake, clear the nearest plant and use vertical cover.
4. Rotate on confirmed sound or contact, not one smoke seen far away.
5. Anti-eco: respect close corners and shotguns.

## Round rule

Call location, number, and direction, then stabilize or rotate with a clear reason.
