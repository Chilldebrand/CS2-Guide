# Vertigo — offense plan

1. Default with several players able to support Ramp and one late lurk.
2. Ramp take: smoke the defensive sightline, molly close corners, and scale as a trade pair.
3. A execute: isolate Heaven/CT and clear close sandbags before planting.
4. B hit: use Mid/Connector pressure to pull the rotate, then enter with a flank watch.
5. If Ramp stalls, fall back before losing the bomb.

## Round rule

Keep the team close enough to trade the first fight. If the first route is denied, preserve the objective and reset.
