# Ancient — defense plan

1. Play one player close enough to help Mid without giving away every round.
2. Use early Mid utility to deny a free walk-up, then fall back into a crossfire rather than fight alone.
3. Keep one B player alive until Mid/Temple information is confirmed; rotations through CT can be slow.
4. On retake, smoke the entry lane, flash the site from safety, and clear the nearest planter first.
5. Against an eco, respect close Cave and Donut swings; rifles should keep distance.

## Round rule

Call location, number, and direction, then stabilize or rotate with a clear reason.
