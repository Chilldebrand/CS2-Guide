# Ancient — offense plan

1. Default with two toward Mid, one A Main, one B Lobby, and one flexible support.
2. Use Mid pressure to threaten Donut/Red Room, then either split A or return to B before CTs can stack.
3. A execute: smoke Temple and CT, flash over A Main, clear close left/right, plant for A Main.
4. B execute: smoke Cave/CT lane, molly close corners, flash through Ramp, and trade the first player into site.
5. If first contact is lost, stop feeding Mid; regroup and hit the opposite lane with the bomb safe.

## Round rule

Keep the team close enough to trade the first fight. If the first route is denied, preserve the bomb and reset.
