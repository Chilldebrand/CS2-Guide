# Shelter — defender plan

1. Anchor the hostage room with two angles and keep one player on the return route.
2. Use circling corridors to delay and reappear, not to chase isolated attackers.
3. After the hostage is taken, attack the escort path from two directions if possible.
4. Focus the carrier first; do not waste the retake on a far escort.
5. Against a rush, fall back to the room and split the entry pair with utility.

## Round rule

The rescue route is the fight. Delay, split the escort, and focus the carrier instead of chasing a distant player.
