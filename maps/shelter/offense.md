# Shelter — attacker plan

1. Use one player to watch the flank, two to clear the approach, and two to support the room entry.
2. Take the closest rooms first and keep a flash for the final doorway.
3. Before moving the hostage, clear the return corridor and identify the defender cut.
4. Put the rifle player ahead of the carrier and keep the second escort close enough to trade.
5. If the room is stacked, use the circling route to force a defender to turn.

## Round rule

Clear the return route before moving the hostage. The carrier is the win condition, so do not abandon the escort for a low-value duel.
