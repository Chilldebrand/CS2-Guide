# Fachwerk — offense plan

1. Default with one long-sightline player, two central players, and two ready to clear the close route.
2. Smoke the outdoor-to-indoor transition, then flash the first room.
3. Pressure one site while the flexible player holds the rotate route.
4. Post-plant positions should cover the entry and defuse approach from different distances.
5. If a new angle catches the team, label it for the next round.

## Round rule

Keep the team close enough to trade the first fight. If the first route is denied, preserve the objective and reset.
