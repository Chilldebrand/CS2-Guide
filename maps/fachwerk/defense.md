# Fachwerk — defense plan

1. Keep a close anchor for indoor routes and a long-range player for the street sightline.
2. Use early utility to prevent a fast transition from street to site.
3. Rotate only after confirming which transition attackers used.
4. Retake with a flash into the indoor choke and a trade player.
5. Anti-eco: clear every doorway and recess before open space.

## Round rule

Call location, number, and direction, then stabilize or rotate with a clear reason.
