# Office utility visual assets

Asset names use `<map>-<role>-<grenade>-<target>-<stage>`, where `<role>` is `attacker` or `defender` and `<stage>` is `start`, `aim`, or `result`.

Each card must preserve the exact target, timing, purpose, and fallback from `maps/office/utility.md`; identify hostage-room access, room isolation, rescue-route effect, safe exit, and anti-rush intent where applicable; and record source, checked date, map version, and permission basis.

Remote images stay source-linked when reuse permission or precise throw behavior is not verified. Do not claim a fixed start, aim, or result from callout geometry alone.
