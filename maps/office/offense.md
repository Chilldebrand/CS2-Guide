# Office — attacker plan

1. Use two players to pressure the main approach, one to clear a side route, and two close enough to trade inside.
2. Take rooms one at a time with flashes; do not split so far that a defender can isolate each attacker.
3. Once the hostage is reached, clear the return route before touching it if time allows.
4. Escort with a rifle in front and a second player watching the carrier’s exposed side.
5. If the first approach is stacked, reset and use the other hallway.

## Round rule

Clear the return route before moving the hostage. The carrier is the win condition, so do not abandon the escort for a low-value duel.
