# Office — defender plan

1. Anchor the hostage room with a crossfire, not five players in one doorway.
2. Use information pushes only with a retreat path; the goal is delay, not donation.
3. When the hostage is taken, fall back to the rescue path and force attackers to clear again.
4. Use utility to split escort from carrier, then focus the carrier.
5. Against a rush, fall back to the room and call the number before re-peeking.

## Round rule

The rescue route is the fight. Delay, split the escort, and focus the carrier instead of chasing a distant player.
