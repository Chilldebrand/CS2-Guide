# Mirage — offense plan

1. Default with two Mid, one A Ramp/Palace, one B Apartments, and one support/flex player.
2. Mid split A: smoke Window/Connector, take Catwalk, flash Short, and combine with A Ramp or Palace.
3. B hit: isolate Market with a smoke, clear Bench/Van, and keep Apartments players close enough to trade.
4. A execute: smoke Jungle/CT, molly Stairs/Under Balcony, and enter behind a flash.
5. If Window is denied, use A pressure to draw a rotate, then split from Palace.

## Round rule

Keep the team close enough to trade the first fight. If the first route is denied, preserve the objective and reset.
