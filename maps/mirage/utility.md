# Mirage — utility priorities

| Target | Timing | Thrower | Purpose | Follow-up / fallback |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| T smoke Window from T Spawn | Mid default | Support | Lets Mid take Top Mid and Connector | If missed, smoke Connector instead |
| T smoke Jungle/CT for A | A execute | Support | Blocks the best retake angles | Plant for Tetris/Default and hold Ramp |
| T smoke Market for B | B hit | Support | Isolates Market | Flash Bench and fight from Apartments |
| CT molly Palace or Ramp | Early defense | A anchor | Delays a fast A hit | Fall back to Stairs and keep Jungle open |
| CT flash Mid from Connector | Mid contest | Mid player | Blinds the first Mid swing | Fall back if the flash misses |
