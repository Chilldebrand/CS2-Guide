# Mirage — defense plan

1. Contest Mid with support and keep a safe A Ramp or Palace reaction.
2. Preserve a Jungle/Connector route for the retake; do not fight from the same box.
3. Apartments info is valuable but a solo push is not mandatory every round.
4. Retake with a flash from Market/CT and clear Bench/Default first.
5. Anti-eco: clear close Palace, Ramp, and Apartments.

## Round rule

Call location, number, and direction, then stabilize or rotate with a clear reason.
