# Anubis — utility priorities

| Target | Timing | Thrower | Purpose | Follow-up / fallback |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| T smoke Connector from Mid | Mid take | Support | Cuts the first rotate and enables a split | If missed, hold Mid and use a flash |
| T smoke Temple/Back Site | B execute | Support | Isolates the deep B hold | Flash Water and plant for the entrance |
| T molly close A boxes | A execute | Entry support | Removes the first close fight | Clear Heaven with a flash |
| CT smoke Mid/Connector | Early defense | Mid player | Delays a fast split and preserves info | Fall back to a crossfire |
| CT flash B Water on contact | B defense | B support | Helps the anchor survive the first swing | Do not re-peek alone |
