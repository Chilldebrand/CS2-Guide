# Anubis — offense plan

1. Default with two Mid/Connector, one A Main, one B Main, and one flexible player with the bomb.
2. Use Mid/Connector to threaten both sites; do not burn every smoke before learning which site is weak.
3. A execute: smoke CT/Heaven, flash through Main, clear close boxes, and plant for the safer lane.
4. B execute: smoke Temple/Back Site, molly close Water/entrance, and enter as a tight trade pair.
5. If the first Connector fight is lost, reset and hit with a late opposite-lane split.

## Round rule

Keep the team close enough to trade the first fight. If the first route is denied, preserve the objective and reset.
