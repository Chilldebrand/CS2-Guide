# Anubis — defense plan

1. Contest Mid with layered utility and a planned retreat; the map punishes isolated early deaths.
2. Keep one B defender alive until Canal/Water information is reliable.
3. Do not give away CT/Heaven at the same time; stagger the first fight and support.
4. Retake through two lanes with a flash and clear the closest planter first.
5. Anti-eco: clear close Water, Connector, and Temple before long fights.

## Round rule

Call location, number, and direction, then stabilize or rotate with a clear reason.
