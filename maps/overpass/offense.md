# Overpass — offense plan

1. Default with Bathrooms/Long pressure, one Short/Connector player, one Monster or Water threat, and a flex bomb carrier.
2. A hit: smoke Heaven/Bank, molly close Truck/Default, and enter from Long with a Connector split.
3. B hit: take Monster and Short together, smoke Heaven/CT, and flash through Water/Construction.
4. Hold Monster/Short crossfires after the plant rather than standing together on site.
5. If Bathrooms is lost, use Monster pressure to pull the A rotator.

## Round rule

Keep the team close enough to trade the first fight. If the first route is denied, preserve the objective and reset.
