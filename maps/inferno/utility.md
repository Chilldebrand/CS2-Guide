# Inferno — utility priorities

| Target | Timing | Thrower | Purpose | Follow-up / fallback |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| CT molly Banana | Early defense | B anchor | Denies free Banana and reveals a rush | Fall back to Car and re-smoke |
| CT smoke Top Banana | After molly | B anchor | Forces more T utility | Hold the smoke edge with a teammate |
| T smoke CT/Coffins | B execute | Support | Removes the strongest site holds | Flash through and plant for Banana |
| T molly New Box/First Oranges | B execute | Entry support | Clears the close plant fight | Plant safe and keep one flash |
| T smoke Arch or Library | A split | Support | Slows the rotate | Pause and re-smoke if missed |
