# Inferno — defense plan

1. Use layered Banana utility: early molly, smoke, then a second piece only on commitment.
2. Communicate Apartments and Brackets separately; do not rotate on one footstep.
3. Retake with Arch/Library or CT together.
4. Save a molotov for the plant or Coffins retake.
5. Anti-eco: respect close Banana and Apartments pushes.

## Round rule

Call location, number, and direction, then stabilize or rotate with a clear reason.
