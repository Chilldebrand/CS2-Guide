# Inferno — offense plan

1. Default with two Banana, one Brackets, one Apartments, and one flexible player with the bomb.
2. Banana hit: clear Logs/Car, smoke CT, molly Coffins/First Oranges, and flash through.
3. A split: pressure Brackets/Apps while Mid players take Top Mid, then smoke Arch/Library.
4. Keep one post-plant player close to stop the tap and one in an Arch/CT trade angle.
5. If Banana stalls, work A rather than repeatedly dry-peeking the choke.

## Round rule

Keep the team close enough to trade the first fight. If the first route is denied, preserve the objective and reset.
