## Positioning visual

![Inferno positioning teaching diagram showing the CT Banana anchor and support, the A anchor, the mid rotator, T pressure on Banana and A side, and the main rotation arrows](assets/positioning-overview.svg)

[Positioning source note](assets/map-overview-source.md)

1. Starting roles: CTs open with a Banana anchor and support, one A anchor, and one Mid rotator while Ts show pressure at Banana and on the A-side route instead of revealing a full commit.
2. Information trigger: the yellow Banana fight zone is the first major decision point; CTs should not over-rotate on one footstep, but confirmed Banana commitment or clean A-side space changes the rotator's job.
3. Rotation/trade path: the white arrows show the main responses from T pressure into Banana or A Short and from the CT rotator or A anchor into the trade path once the call is clear.

## Win condition

Make Banana or Apartments expensive to contest, then use the rotation pressure to isolate the other site.

## Learn first

1. Learn common callouts and safe routes.
2. Play the default for five rounds before changing it.
3. Practice the utility targets with a teammate.
4. Review one spacing or timing error after the match.

## Five-player defaults

These are opening-role overlays over the sourced map overview. Use the T diagram to assign routes and initial pressure; use the CT diagram to assign hold angles and the first rotation trigger. They are teaching overlays, not pixel-perfect radars.

### T-side default

![Inferno T-side five-player default showing roles, spawn-based routes, and initial pressure](assets/default-t.svg)

Keep the first route close enough to trade. If the pressure point is denied, preserve the bomb and regroup rather than feeding another isolated fight.

### CT-side default

![Inferno CT-side five-player default showing roles, hold angles, support, and the first rotation trigger](assets/default-ct.svg)

Call location, number, and direction before rotating. Hold the shown lane until reliable information changes the job.
