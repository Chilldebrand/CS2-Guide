# Italy — attacker plan

1. Default with one player watching the long flank, two pressuring upper, and two supporting the room take.
2. Clear alleys with flashes and keep the second player close enough to trade the first doorway.
3. Once the room is secure, send one player ahead on the return route before the carrier moves.
4. Use the balcony route to split defender attention, not to take a solo fight.
5. If defenders turtle, use fire and smokes to make them move.

## Round rule

Clear the return route before moving the hostage. The carrier is the win condition, so do not abandon the escort for a low-value duel.
