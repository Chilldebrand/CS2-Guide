# Train — defense plan

1. Use Yard utility to collect information without giving a free AWP kill.
2. Keep an anchor on the site that is not pressured; rotations through Connector are valuable.
3. Fight around train cover with a teammate and avoid chasing through ladders.
4. Retakes need a clear order: nearest plant, nearest train, then the deeper angle.
5. Anti-eco: clear close ladders and corners.

## Round rule

Call location, number, and direction, then stabilize or rotate with a clear reason.
