# Train — offense plan

1. Default with Yard pressure, one Ivy/Popdog threat, and a player able to join either site.
2. Use Yard smokes to cross, then pressure Connector/Inner so the defense cannot hold both entrances.
3. A hit: isolate Hell/Connector, flash through Main, and trade across train cover.
4. B hit: take Lower/Upper together, smoke the strongest retake lane, and plant for survivors.
5. If Yard is denied, slow the round and attack one lane with full utility.

## Round rule

Keep the team close enough to trade the first fight. If the first route is denied, preserve the objective and reset.
