# Nuke — offense plan

1. Default with Outside pressure, one Lobby player, one Ramp/Secret threat, and a bomb carrier who can join the read.
2. Outside split: smoke crossing gaps, take Secret, and force lower defenders to respect B before an A hit.
3. Ramp hit: clear Hut/Heaven routes, smoke Double/Window, and enter as a trade pair.
4. A hit: smoke Heaven/CT, molly close Hut/Default, and watch Squeaky/rotate.
5. If the defense gives Outside, use that space to make the CT setup rotate instead of rushing lower blindly.

## Round rule

Keep the team close enough to trade the first fight. If the first route is denied, preserve the objective and reset.
