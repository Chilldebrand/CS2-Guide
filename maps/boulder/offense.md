# Boulder — offense plan

1. Start with a three-lane default and identify which central route the defense gives up.
2. Take the central route with two players, then choose the safer site and plant for survivors.
3. Use one smoke for the longest sightline and one flash for the first close fight.
4. Keep the bomb central until first contact is understood.
5. After a failed hit, write down the callout and angle rather than repeating it.

## Round rule

Keep the team close enough to trade the first fight. If the first route is denied, preserve the objective and reset.
