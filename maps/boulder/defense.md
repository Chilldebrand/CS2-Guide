# Boulder — defense plan

1. Use a two-player anchor plus one flexible central player; keep the other site from becoming free.
2. Deny the direct central route while preserving a retake smoke.
3. Confirm callouts with teammates in warm-up; do not assume workshop names are universal.
4. Retake from two lanes and clear the nearest plant.
5. Against an eco, clear new off-angles before long fights.

## Round rule

Call location, number, and direction, then stabilize or rotate with a clear reason.
