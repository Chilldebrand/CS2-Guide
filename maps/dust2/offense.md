# Dust II — offense plan

1. Default with two Long, two Mid/Short, and one B Tunnel or lurk player.
2. Long take: smoke Cross, flash over Long, clear Pit/Goose, and watch the CT rotation.
3. Mid-to-B split: smoke Xbox, take Cat, and hit Short with Long pressure.
4. B hit: smoke CT, flash Back Site/Window, clear close Tunnel, and plant for survivors.
5. If Long is lost, use Mid or B Tunnel instead of repeating dry swings.

## Round rule

Keep the team close enough to trade the first fight. If the first route is denied, preserve the objective and reset.
