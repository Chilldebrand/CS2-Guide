# Dust II — utility priorities

| Target | Timing | Thrower | Purpose | Follow-up / fallback |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| T smoke Cross from Long | Long take | Support | Allows Long players to cross | Hold Pit and wait for the second flash |
| T smoke Xbox | Mid split | Mid player | Blocks CT/AWP while Cat climbs | If missed, use a flash and take Lower Tunnels |
| T smoke CT on B | B hit | Support | Cuts the fastest rotate and retake | Plant for Tunnel and save one flash |
| CT smoke Long Cross | Early defense | Long anchor | Delays a full Long take | Fall back to Pit if spammed |
| CT molly B Tunnels | Anti-rush | B anchor | Stalls first contact and buys rotation | Play close only after fire clears |

### T smoke Cross from Long

**Side / role:** T / Support<br>
**Timing:** Long take<br>
**Purpose:** Allows Long players to cross

1. **Start:** Stand on the start of the boxes behind the car outside Long Doors, as shown in the remote guide. [Remote source - not stored locally](https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3400657113)
2. **Aim:** Aim above the tree leaves, offset slightly up and right from the source's reference, then jump-throw. [Remote source - not stored locally](https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3400657113)
3. **Result:** The smoke covers the A Cross sightline from CT so Long players can cross while the support watches for spam or an HE opening. [Remote source - not stored locally](https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3400657113)

**Fallback:** Hold Pit and wait for the second flash
