# Dust II — defense plan

1. Contest Long with two players or concede it cleanly; half-committing creates isolated deaths.
2. Use Mid utility to stop an early Cat walk-up and keep one player listening for B Tunnel.
3. Rotate through CT/Short only after a reliable call.
4. Retake B with a Close/Window split and a flash.
5. Anti-eco: keep range, clear Goose/Close Long, and watch Cat/Tunnel.

## Round rule

Call location, number, and direction, then stabilize or rotate with a clear reason.
