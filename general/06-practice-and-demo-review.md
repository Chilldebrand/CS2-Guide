# Practice and demo review

## A 30–45 minute routine

1. **5 minutes:** counter-strafe and burst at a wall or aim map.
2. **10 minutes:** deathmatch; prioritize head height and stopping before shooting.
3. **10 minutes:** one map walk; practice callouts and three utility targets.
4. **10 minutes:** retake or execute practice with a friend/bot.
5. **5 minutes:** write one behavior to carry into the next match.

## Demo review worksheet

For three deaths, write:

- What information did I have?
- What did I believe the opponent would do?
- Was my spacing tradeable?
- Did utility change the fight?
- Was the death caused by mechanics, spacing, information, timing, or economy?
- What exact rule will I try next match?

Review losses at 0.5× speed only when the situation is confusing. The purpose is to change a behavior, not to watch the whole match again.
