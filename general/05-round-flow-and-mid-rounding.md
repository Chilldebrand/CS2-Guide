# Round flow and mid-rounding

## T side

Start with a default that gathers information and preserves options. Once a defender gives information, choose one of three branches:

- **Hit:** commit through a cleared route before the defense resets.
- **Split:** use control from two connected areas to stretch rotations.
- **Reset:** fall back, keep the bomb safe, and force the defense to spend or reveal utility again.

Do not call a late execute without enough time to clear the first choke point and plant.

## CT side

Take early space only when the utility and trade plan exist. Once information arrives, stabilize: keep one player on the other site, preserve a route for the retake, and do not rotate three players on one uncertain call.

## Post-plant

Plant for the teammates who are alive, not for the most common default. Build a crossfire with one player close enough to stop the defuse and another positioned to trade the first contact.
