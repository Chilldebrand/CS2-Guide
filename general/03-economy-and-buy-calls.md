# Economy and buy calls

## Simple team calls

- **Full buy:** rifles/SMGs, armor, grenades, and the team can contest the round.
- **Force buy:** spend into a weak economy to surprise the opponent or protect a lead; call it explicitly.
- **Half buy:** spend enough for a useful weapon and a few grenades while preserving a future full buy.
- **Save:** keep money for the next round; do not die with an expensive rifle in a lost fight.

## Purchase priorities

1. Weapon and armor appropriate to the round.
2. At least one flash or smoke that supports the planned fight.
3. Defuse kit on CT when the team can afford it.
4. Molotov/incendiary for a choke point or post-plant denial.

The exact cash threshold changes with the scoreboard and surviving weapons. The important skill is synchronized spending, not memorizing one number.

## Anti-eco rules

Expect close-range pistols, stacks, and unarmored surprise swings. Stay together, clear corners, keep distance, and do not hand the opponent a rifle. If you have a rifle and the enemy has pistols, your job is to make the round boring.
